The Giving
Coffee Initiative

coffee+

Coffee+ is more than a cup of coffee. It is a purpose-led initiative created to turn everyday moments into meaningful impact. With every purchase, Coffee+ supports year-round giving, helping fund vital humanitarian efforts while delivering coffee you can feel good about choosing.

We believe that doing good should be simple, consistent, and part of everyday life. That's why Coffee+ was created to make impact accessible, one sip at a time.

Our journey started with a simple question: What if the coffee we drink every morning could help someone in need? By partnering with organizations like the Canadian Red Cross, we ensure that every bean roasted translates into actionable support for communities facing crises.

Sustainably Sourced

Our blends are crafted using premium Ethiopian Arabica beans, sourced responsibly from farming communities known for their rich coffee heritage and exceptional quality. Every roast is selected to deliver depth of flavour while honouring the people and places behind each harvest.
