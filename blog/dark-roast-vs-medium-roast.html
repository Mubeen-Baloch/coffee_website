Dark roast vs medium roast

Roast level changes how heat transforms sugars and acids inside the bean. It is not a simple “stronger caffeine” dial — extraction, dose, and grind size usually move the needle more than roast colour alone.

What dark roast tastes like

Darker roasts emphasise roast-derived flavours: chocolate, smoke, caramel, and a heavier body. Coffee+ Extravaganza is our deep, intense expression for people who want a commanding cup.

What medium roast preserves

Medium roasts keep more of the bean’s origin character — fruit, florals, or tea-like notes depending on terroir. Nostalgia sits in the balanced, comforting middle; Energizer pushes slightly darker for more momentum.

Pick by use case

  • Late-night coding → often Extravaganza (if you accept evening caffeine).
  • All-day office mug → Nostalgia.
  • Study sprint → Energizer.
