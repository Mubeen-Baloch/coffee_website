Best coffee for programmers

Long compile times, context switches, and midnight deploys reward predictable rituals. The “best” coffee is the one you can brew consistently, enjoy black or with milk, and stop drinking before it steals sleep from tomorrow’s you.

Roast choice for long nights

Many developers like dark roasts for lower perceived acidity during all-nighters. Extravaganza delivers intensity; Energizer offers a medium-dark motivator if you want boldness with a slightly different curve. Compare in dark vs medium roast.

Desk ergonomics meet mug ergonomics

Use a thermal mug to avoid reheating (which can flatten aromatics), stand between PRs, and keep water next to the IDE. Coffee supports focus; movement preserves it.
