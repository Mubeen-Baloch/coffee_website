Decaf coffee guide

Great decaf should taste like coffee, not like compromise. Modern decaffeination aims to strip caffeine while keeping volatile aromatics intact — quality in still matters: storage, grind freshness, and water chemistry.

When decaf wins

Evening meetings in Blue Area, post-gym wind-down, or simply breaking caffeine dependency while keeping ritual — decaf belongs in serious pantries. Pair with caffeine timing discipline.
